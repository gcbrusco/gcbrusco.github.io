Intern Spring 2019 - Present

Engineering tomorrow is an educational organization that exposes engineering disciplines to high school students all around the nation. The organization creates both in person and virtual labs on a variety of topics, including machine learning, embedded systems, network theory, and app development.

MY ROLE: During the summer and winter time, I collaborate with the ET team to create and fix labs for school use, specifically the embedded systems and machine learning labs. HIGHLIGHT: My favorite part about working with Engineering Tomorrow is seeing the growth of the organization. During the Summer of 2019, I was one of three interns, and now there are over a dozen!
