WHERE I AM FROM

As you can tell by the theme, I am a die-hard New Yorker. I come from a large, full-blooded Italian family, where almost all of my relatives live in either New York or Italy. So, yes. I am one of those "fogetaboutit" New York Italians with mandatory sunday dinners, traditional Catholic values, and an exaggerated use of hand gestures. I am the fourth born child of five, where I grew up with two brothers and two sisters. Family of seven – I know – it was just as chaotic as it seems.

WHAT I LIKE TO DO

As you can see with the images on this site, one of my main interests is graphic design. Ever since my 10th grade art class in high school, I have been obsessed with drawing, painting, and digital design. A few art classes and contests later, this is unfortunately just a hobby to me now. However, I believe computer science requires just as much creativity as art does, where each empty terminal window is the same as a blank canvas.
